How To Make A Sandwich

  1. Get Out Bread
  2. Open Bread Bag
  3. Get Out 2 Knives
  4. Get Out Peanut Butter
  5. Open Peanut Butter Jar
  6. Get Out Jelly
  7. Open Jelly Jar
  8. Get Out a knife
  9. Lay Out 2 Slices of Bread
  10. Dip Knife into Peanut Butter
  11. Spread Peanut Butter onto Bread
  12. Dip a Clean Knife into Jelly
  13. Spread Jelly onto Other Slice of Bread
  14. Combine both slices of Bread with Peanut Butter and Jelly Facing Each Other
